kite Runner Book by Khaled hossian

The Kite Runner is a powerful and emotional novel that explores friendship, guilt, and redemption. Set mainly in Afghanistan, the story follows Amir, a young boy from a wealthy family, and his close friend Hassan, who is the son of his father’s servant. Their bond is tested by betrayal and the political turmoil in their country.

A Thousand Splendid Suns

A Thousand Splendid Suns is a deeply emotional novel that tells the story of two Afghan women, Mariam and Laila, whose lives become tragically connected through war, loss, and marriage. Set against the backdrop of Afghanistan’s turbulent history—from the Soviet invasion to Taliban rule—the novel shows how political conflict affects ordinary lives, especially women.Mariam, an illegitimate child, grows up facing rejection and hardship, while Laila is raised with hope and education but later loses everything to war. Though they begin as rivals, their shared suffering turns them into strong allies. Their bond highlights themes of female friendship, sacrifice, resilience, and love.Hosseini’s writing is simple yet powerful, making the pain and strength of the characters feel real and personal. The novel exposes the injustice faced by women while also celebrating their courage and endurance.
